# Timer

Boost your focus and productivity with the built-in Pomodoro timer.  
This tool helps you break your work into focused intervals, separated by short breaks — a proven method to stay engaged without burning out.

---

## Core Features

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="Work in Focused Sprints" icon="clock">
    Choose from preset durations to quickly start:

    - 25-minute Pomodoro session  
    - 5-minute short break  
    - 15-minute long break

    These defaults make it easy to stay in rhythm throughout your day.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Custom Timers" icon="sliders">
    Need something more flexible?

    - Set your own durations for both focus and break intervals  
    - Perfect for personal workflows or alternative productivity techniques
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Desktop Notifications" icon="bell">
    Get a system notification when a timer ends — so you know when to pause or resume.

    - Requires notification permission in your browser  
    - Works in most modern environments
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
